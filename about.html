That influence the way we work and help us realise the impact we wish to achieve.

Human Centered

Our approach is human centred design, putting the user in the centre of everything we do. It helps us ensure that our solutions are built for adoption, and are driven by true user value.

“There is nothing worse than the sharp image of a fuzzy concept.”

- Ansel Adams

Thinkers & Doers

We are both thinkers and doers. We imagine create ideas and then make sure they are implemented successfully. Taking the risk out of design and innovation.

Generative/Iterative

Creative-Analytic

We are a creative team, nested in the hard-nosed business and technology domains. Such a multidisciplinary nature brings rigour and reliability to imagination. Creating a unique and highly valuable competence.

Emotional-Functional

We strive to crafting remarkable solutions that don’t just deliver functional results, but also elicit emotions. Solutions that drive a positive user experience.

Design Sells

We believe that the true value of innovation and design, lies in its successful adoption. In 2018, we won 9 global design awards including the prestigious Red Dot Award, but our products also delivered 700 Million USD for our clients businesses. That is the true measure of our success.

Culture Vulture

The Culture Vulture program is a collective run by the Future Factory community. The program aims at nurturing the shared values that are fundamental to the Future Factory DNA, and strongly influences how we operate. The collective helps articulate who we are and what we stand for, and assists members in embracing our naturally collaborative, positive and entrepreneurial culture. The following 5 principles lay the foundation of our culture:

Prioritise the collective

We operate as networks, working collaboratively across disciplines and hierarchies. The community is what we hold dearest, implicit in the faith that we draw our best work from collaboration.

Be optimistic

Design is about positive imagination. To be innovative is to believe in a positive outcome, to envision a better world. Optimism guides everything we do. We have our head in the clouds but our feet on the ground.

Be comfortable with grey

Innovation is not black or white. It is more about what might be, rather than what we know for certain. At Future Factory, we encourage a spirit of being comfortable with the unknown, as we navigate the uncertainty towards a positive conviction.

Have ownership

Our culture is rooted on ownership. We are an outrageously flat hierarchy, made up of individuals who take complete charge of what they do. It is the only way for the collective to survive.

Be resilient

The Future Factory community is strong hearted. We are people who can take pressure without passing it on. We are people who see tough situations through to the end. We are innovators driven to create a brave new world.
