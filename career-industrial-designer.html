Industrial Designer

Who we are

We are an innovation company, passionate about our work and convinced about creating a better world by design. Despite being one of India’s largest design companies, in reality we work as a close-knit group of people driven to create impact through our work. We are recipients of the Red Dot, IDSA, Good Design awards and our new research has been published in Europe and Asia. You should apply to us if you:

  • Are full of ideas that you are convinced will change the world
  • Driven to design thinking yet equally happy working with your hands in a studio environment
  • Are comfortable engaging with teams across Design, Technology and Business
  • Thrive in a challenging environment, can handle pressure and enjoy managing work and expectations
  • Believe in the potential of design & are an avid advocate of it

“A place that always gives you challenging work to push your limits and helps you get the best out of yourself.”

“It is invigorating to work with a bunch of creative people who are unafraid of thinking different.”

Key responsibilities
  • Working on insights / ideas as part of a team to develop design concepts
  • Taking part in specialist or multidisciplinary team meetings / brainstorming sessions
  • Extensive sketching for ideation and design
  • Making various prototypes / samples / working models in the prototyping workshop
Job requirements
  • Excellent Design skills both in thought and execution
  • Experience of 0-5 years in a reputed design firm
  • Good on-ground experience
  • Excellent written and verbal communication and presentation skills
  • Past work experience that showcase skills in the expected responsibilities
